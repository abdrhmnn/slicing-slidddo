Is this a subscription service?

No. Currently, we are working as a one-time payment service. This means no monthly payments. Also, you will get free updates as long as slidddo will be alive.

Can i customize the slides?

Of course! You can customize absolutely all elements on the slide such as colors, shapes, images, and more.

What the files inside?

There are PPTX files for PowerPoint and Google Slides, KEY files for Keynote, and FIG files for Figma.

What kind of license will i get?

You may modify the slides according to your requirements and use them in any of your personal and commercial projects. You do not have the rights to redistribute, resell, lease, license, sub-license or offer the file downloaded to any third party.

How i get the slides after purchase?

After purchase you can download the slides and assets for them via Dropbox. Very simple and fast!

Are the images included?

Yes! Images are not inserted in the slides by default, but you will get access to download and paste them by yourself.
